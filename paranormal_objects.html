PO-1

Description: PO-1 is a tall, short-legged, creepy, human-like figure with long arms. Its unsettling appearance is amplified by its ability to move swiftly despite its strange proportions.

Containment: PO-1 is contained in a secure, high-tech containment chamber designed to prevent physical contact. Special surveillance equipment is used to monitor its movements at all times.

Special Containment Operations: PO-1 is currently contained in Area 5. Only Class B and above personnel are permitted access to PO-1.

PO-3

Description: PO-3 is a virus that appears to have its own mind. It can kill anyone the moment they are infected. The virus spreads rapidly and aggressively, making it one of the most dangerous objects in containment.

Containment: PO-3 is contained in a high-security, biohazard chamber in Area 5. The containment area is equipped with air filtration systems and automated lockdown protocols to prevent exposure.

Special Containment Operations: PO-3 is currently contained in Area 5. Only Class R personnel are authorized to access PO-3 due to its extreme lethality.

Ranks of FoPO

The following are the ranks of the Foundation of Paranormal Objects, from highest to lowest:

  1. Class R - Highest rank, granted to personnel with the authority to handle the most dangerous and classified objects.
  2. Class A - Senior personnel allowed access to highly dangerous objects and operations.
  3. Class B - Mid-level personnel with access to certain dangerous objects, restricted operations.
  4. Class C - Junior personnel with limited access to less hazardous objects, under supervision.
  5. Class E - Entry-level personnel, typically on probation or engaged in basic tasks.
  6. Class D - Lowest rank, typically temporary or subject to severe restrictions due to lack of clearance.
